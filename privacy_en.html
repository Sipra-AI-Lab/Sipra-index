Sipra Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 26, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Sipra is committed to protecting your privacy. We collect the following information:

  • Beverage Data: Water intake, caffeine consumption, alcohol consumption, sipration record timestamps
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifier
  • Usage Data: App usage frequency, feature usage statistics

All data is stored locally on your device. We do not upload your personal beverage data to our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide core app features (sipration tracking, statistics, energy recommendations)
  • Sync data between your iPhone, Apple Watch, and Widgets (using App Groups)
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Generate personalized beverage & energy reports

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal beverage data.

We may share data only in the following situations:

  • With your explicit consent
  • When required by law or to protect our rights
  • With trusted third-party service providers (operational necessity only, subject to strict confidentiality obligations)

4. Data Security

We employ multiple security measures to protect your information:

  • Local encrypted storage for all data
  • iOS Keychain for sensitive information protection
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Compliance with Apple data security best practices

5. Third-Party Services

Sipra may use the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store: App distribution and in-app purchase processing
  • StoreKit: Subscription management (Apple's official payment system)

These services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing their terms.

6. Caffeine & Alcohol Tracking Disclaimer

Important Notice:

  • BAC (Blood Alcohol Content) calculations are for reference only and do not constitute medical or legal advice
  • Individual metabolic rates vary; calculated results may differ from actual values
  • This app does not encourage excessive drinking. Please drink responsibly
  • Never drink and drive. Always obey local laws and regulations

7. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: View all data within the app at any time
  • Delete: Remove all data through app settings
  • Export: Request a data copy via email
  • Opt-Out: Uninstall the app to stop data collection

8. Children's Privacy

Sipra is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will immediately delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. For significant changes, we will notify you via in-app notification or email. Continued use of the app indicates acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact us:

  • Email: support@sipra.vip
  • In-App: Settings → Help & Feedback

This privacy policy is effective as of January 26, 2025.
